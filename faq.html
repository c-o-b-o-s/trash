Frequently Asked Questions

What are the days?

  • Basic: Every Thursday
  • Standard: Every Monday and Thursday
  • Premium: Every Monday, Thursday, and Saturday

Is there a set time?

Please set your trash outside of your door no earlier than 7pm and no later than 9pm on pickup days.

Is my personal and payment information safe?

Absolutely. All payments are processed securely through Stripe, a leading global payment platform trusted by millions of businesses. We never store your card details directly, and your personal information (like your address) is kept private and only used to provide service. Your data is encrypted and protected end-to-end.

What is included?

All subscriptions include weekly trash services. We also spray Febreze in the hallway to keep things smelling fresh.

What if I forget to put my trash out?

We send out text reminders on trash days, but if you forget - No worries...just hold onto it until the next pickup day. To keep things fair for everyone, we can’t come back outside the scheduled hours.

What is the cancellation policy?

There are no long-term contracts. You can manage or cancel your subscription anytime through our billing portal: your Stripe billing portal .
